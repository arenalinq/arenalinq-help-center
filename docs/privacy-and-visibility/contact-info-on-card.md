# 9.2.2 Contact Info on Your Card

Athletes 18 and older have two optional toggles in **Settings → Permissions**:

- **Show my email on my public card** — displays your login email directly on your card.
- **Show my phone number on my public card** — displays your phone number on your card.

Both toggles are **off by default**. When turned on, anyone who views your card can see the information. You can turn them off again at any time.

:::caution Under 18
Athletes under 18 do not have these toggles. Contact information is never displayed on a minor's public card.
:::
