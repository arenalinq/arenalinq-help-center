# 9.2.1 Visibility Settings

You control who can see your profile. There are three visibility levels:

- **Private** (default) — Only you (and your parent, if applicable) can see your profile. Scouts cannot find you in searches.
- **Scouts Only** — Verified coaching staff on Arenalinq can view your profile. It is not visible to the general public.
- **Public** — Anyone with your profile URL can view your card.

## How to change your visibility

1. Go to **Settings → Permissions → Profile Visibility**.
2. Select the level you want.

:::info Paid feature
**Scouts Only** and **Public** require a Premium plan ($4.99/month). The **Private** tier is always free. See [Plans & Pricing](/athlete-billing/plans-and-pricing) for details.
:::
