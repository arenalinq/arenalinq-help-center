# 9.2.3 Share Links

Share links let you send your profile to specific people without making it publicly visible.

## How it works

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Share Link**.
2. Click **Generate share link**.
3. You'll get a unique URL with a token — copy it and send it to whoever you'd like (e.g., a college coach).

Anyone with the link can view your full profile, even if your visibility is set to **Private**.

## Revoking a link

If you want to stop an existing link from working, generate a new one. The old link is immediately revoked and will no longer load your profile.

:::tip
Share links are a great way to get your profile in front of college coaches without opening it up to the entire internet.
:::
