# 7.3 Managing Your Subscription

To manage your subscription, update your payment method, or view invoices:

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. In the **Billing** section, click through to the billing portal.

This opens a secure **Stripe-hosted billing portal** where you can:

- Update your payment method
- View invoices and payment history
- Cancel your subscription

If you add or remove team members on the Program plan, your billing adjusts automatically with proration.
