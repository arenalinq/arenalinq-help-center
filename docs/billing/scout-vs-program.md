# 7.1 Scout vs Program Plans

**Scout — $100/year**

One seat. Designed for individual coaches. Includes pipeline management with up to 4 custom stages and up to 1,000 players. Does not include player imports, shared notes, or team collaboration.

**Program — $200/seat/year**

Up to 5 seats. Designed for coaching staffs. Includes everything in Scout, plus player imports (CSV/Excel), shared notes, activity history, unlimited pipeline stages, up to 10,000 players, and the ability to invite and collaborate with your staff.
