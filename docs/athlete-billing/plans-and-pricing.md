# 9.4.1 Plans & Pricing

Arenalinq offers two tiers for athletes:

## Free

- Create your full profile (bio, attributes, experience, highlights, social links).
- Share your profile via a [private share link](/privacy-and-visibility/share-links).
- Your profile is **Private** by default — only you can see it.

## Premium — $4.99/month

- Everything in Free, plus:
- Unlock **Scouts Only** and **Public** visibility settings.
- Appear in scout searches so coaching staff can discover you.
- Show your profile to the recruiting world.

## What happens if you cancel

Your profile stays fully intact — nothing is deleted. Visibility reverts to **Private** at the end of your billing period. You keep all your data and can re-subscribe anytime.
