# 9.4.2 Managing Your Subscription

## Upgrading to Premium

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade to Premium**.
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe checkout to enter your payment details.

Once complete, you can change your visibility to Scouts Only or Public right away.

## Managing payment or canceling

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Stripe customer portal.

From the portal you can update your payment method, view invoices, or cancel your subscription.

## Billing status

Your current status is displayed on the Billing tab:

- **Free** — no active subscription.
- **Premium** — active subscription, renews automatically.
- **Premium — canceling** — subscription is active until the displayed end date, then reverts to Free.
