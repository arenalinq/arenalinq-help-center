# 9.1.1 Creating Your Profile

Getting started on Arenalinq takes just a few minutes.

**To create your athlete profile:**

1. Go to [app.arenalinq.com/athletes](https://app.arenalinq.com/athletes) and tap **Get Started**.
2. Enter your email address. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code — enter it to continue. There are no passwords on Arenalinq; you'll use this same email-and-code flow every time you sign in.
3. Walk through the signup wizard:
   - **Name** — your first and last name.
   - **Date of birth** — this determines your age tier and privacy defaults.
   - **Sport** — choose your sport (e.g., soccer).
   - **Position(s)** — select your primary and secondary positions.
   - **Graduation year** — your expected high school graduation year.

Once complete, you'll land on your profile editor where you can start filling in the details.

:::note Under 13?
If the athlete is under 13, a parent or guardian must create the profile on their behalf. During signup the athlete will be prompted to enter a parent's email address. See [Parent Accounts](/family-and-parents/parent-account) for details.
:::
