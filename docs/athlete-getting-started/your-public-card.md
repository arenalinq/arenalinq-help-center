# 9.1.3 Your Public Card

Your public card is how scouts and the outside world see your profile. Think of it as your recruiting business card.

## Where to find it

Your card lives at **arenalinq.com/yourusername** (for example, `arenalinq.com/jdoe2028`).

## What's displayed

- Name and photo
- Position(s)
- Bio
- Experience (team history)
- Education
- Highlight videos
- Social links

The exact information shown depends on your [visibility settings](/privacy-and-visibility/visibility-settings) and your age tier. For example, athletes under 15 have limited details visible to protect their privacy.

## Sharing your card

Even if your profile is set to **Private**, you can share it with specific people using a share link. Go to **Settings → Account → Share Link** to generate a unique URL you can send directly to college coaches. See [Share Links](/privacy-and-visibility/share-links) for more.
