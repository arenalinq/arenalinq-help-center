# 9.1.2 Profile Editor

The profile editor is where you build out everything scouts and coaches will see. It's organized into sections you can fill in at your own pace.

## Sections

| Section | What to add |
|---|---|
| **Bio** | Your name, preferred name (nickname), and a short bio about yourself as a player. |
| **Attributes** | Height, weight, preferred foot, and nationality. |
| **Experience** | Team history — add each club or school team with start and end dates. |
| **Education** | Your school name and class/graduation year. |
| **Highlights** | Video links from YouTube or other platforms showcasing your play. |
| **Social** | Your handles for Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, and YouTube. |

## How to edit

Click any field and start typing. Changes save automatically when you click away — no save button needed.

## Photo upload

Click your avatar at the top of the editor to upload a headshot. A clear, recent photo helps coaches put a face to your profile.
