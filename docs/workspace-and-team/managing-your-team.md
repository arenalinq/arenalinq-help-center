# 6.3 Managing Your Team

:::info Program Plan Only
Team management is available on the **Program plan** only.
:::

From the **Scouts** page, you can see all current members and pending invitations.

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything. One owner per workspace. Can manage billing, settings, members, and delete players. |
| **Admin** | Can invite and manage members, manage billing, and access settings. |
| **Member** | Default role for invited users. Can view and edit players and the pipeline. |

You can remove a team member from the Scouts page. When a member is removed, your seat count decreases and billing adjusts automatically with proration.
