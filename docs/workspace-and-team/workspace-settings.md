# 6.1 Workspace Settings

Your workspace is your team's shared recruiting environment. To access settings, click **Settings** in the sidebar. Settings are available to workspace **owners** and **admins** only.

From workspace settings you can:

- Update your workspace name
- Upload a workspace profile photo (5 MB max, 256Ã—256 recommended)
- View and manage your current plan and billing
- Access the Stripe billing portal

:::note
Your conference is set during onboarding and cannot be changed from the settings page. If you need to update your conference, contact us at support@arenalinq.com.
:::
