# 6.2 Inviting Staff (Scouts)

:::info Program Plan Only
Team collaboration is available on the **Program plan** only.
:::

You can invite members of your coaching staff to collaborate in your workspace.

1. Click **Scouts** in the sidebar.
2. Click **"Invite members"**.
3. Enter their email address.
4. They'll receive an email invitation with a link to join your workspace.

Once they accept the invitation and sign in with their OTP code, they'll have access to your shared pipeline and player data.
