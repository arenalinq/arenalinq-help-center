# 5.2 Available Filter Options

- **Location** — Filter by player's hometown or region
- **Club Level** — Filter by club or team level
- **Graduation Year** — Filter by expected graduation year
- **Position(s)** — Filter by player position
- **Gender** — Filter by gender

:::tip
Filters are especially useful when preparing for recruiting events, reviewing a specific graduating class, or narrowing your board to a particular position group.
:::
