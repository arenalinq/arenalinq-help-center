# 9.3.1 Parent Accounts

If your child is under 13, a parent or guardian must create and manage their profile.

## How it works

1. Your child begins the signup wizard at [app.arenalinq.com/athletes](https://app.arenalinq.com/athletes) and enters their date of birth.
2. Because they're under 13, the wizard asks for a **parent email address**.
3. You'll receive an invite link at that email. Click it to create your parent account.
4. From there, you manage your child's profile through **Settings → Family**.

## What parents can do

- Edit the child's profile (bio, attributes, experience, highlights, etc.)
- Change visibility settings
- Manage billing

## When your child turns 13+

Once your child is 13 or older, they can claim ownership of their profile using a claim code. See [Claim Codes](/family-and-parents/claim-codes) for how that works.
