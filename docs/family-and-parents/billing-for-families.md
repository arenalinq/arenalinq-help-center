# 9.3.3 Billing for Families

Every athlete profile on Arenalinq is **free to create**. You can build out the full profile — bio, experience, highlights, social links — and share it via a private link at no cost.

The **Premium plan ($4.99/month)** unlocks visibility to scouts by enabling the Scouts Only and Public visibility settings.

## Managing billing as a parent

Parents can manage billing for their child's profile from **Settings → Billing**. This works the same as any other subscription:

- Click **Upgrade to Premium** to start a subscription.
- Click **Manage Subscription** to update payment methods, view invoices, or cancel.

Payment is handled securely through Stripe.

## Canceling

You can cancel anytime. Your child's profile stays intact, but visibility reverts to **Private** at the end of the current billing period. You can re-subscribe whenever you're ready.
