# 9.3.2 Claim Codes

When a parent creates a profile for a child, the child can later claim ownership of it.

## How to claim a profile

1. The child must be **13 or older**.
2. The parent generates a **claim code** from **Settings → Family**.
3. The child starts their own signup at [app.arenalinq.com/athletes](https://app.arenalinq.com/athletes) and enters the claim code when prompted.
4. The child completes signup with their own email address.

## After claiming

- The child can now sign in with their own email and edit their profile directly.
- The child's email becomes the primary login for the account.
- The parent retains **read-only access** — they can still view the profile but can no longer make edits.
