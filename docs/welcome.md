# 1. Welcome to Arenalinq

Arenalinq is a recruiting platform built for college athletics. It gives coaching staffs a centralized place to manage their recruiting pipeline â€” tracking prospective student-athletes from first contact through commitment.

Instead of juggling spreadsheets, email threads, and scattered notes, Arenalinq brings everything into one workspace your entire staff can access.

## 1.1 What You Can Do

- Organize prospects on a visual pipeline board with customizable stages
- Import your existing recruit lists from CSV or Excel files (Program plan)
- Track every recruit's status as they move through your evaluation process
- Filter your pipeline by position, graduation year, location, and more
- Invite your coaching staff and collaborate in a shared workspace (Program plan)

This help center will walk you through everything you need to get started.
