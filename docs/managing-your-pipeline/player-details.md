# 4.4 Player Details

Click on any player card to open the **Player Detail Panel**. This is where you view and edit all of a player's information.

## Detail Panel Sections

- **Player Card** — Avatar, name, and key identifiers at a glance.
- **Highlights** — A quick snapshot of standout information.
- **Info** — Nationality, hometown, date of birth, position(s), preferred foot, graduation year, and GPA.
- **Experience** — Playing history and background.
- **Education** — Academic details.
- **Contact** — Email, phone, and other contact info.
- **Social** — Links to Twitter, Instagram, and video highlights.
- **Footer** — Shows who last updated the player and when.

All fields save automatically when you click away or press Enter — there's no save button to worry about.

:::tip Deleting a Player
Workspace owners can delete a player using the overflow menu (⋯) on the player card. You'll be asked to confirm before the player is removed.
:::
