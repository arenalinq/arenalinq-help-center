# 9.5 Athlete FAQ

### Can scouts see my email or phone number?

Only if you're 18 or older **and** you turn on the toggle in **Settings → Permissions**. Both toggles are off by default. Under-18 athletes never have contact info displayed.

### Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to **Settings → Account → Danger Zone** and follow the prompts. This permanently deletes your profile and all associated data.

### What if I'm under 13?

A parent or guardian must create your profile on your behalf. Once you turn 13, you can [claim the profile](/family-and-parents/claim-codes) with a code from your parent.

### Can I change my date of birth?

No. Your date of birth is set during signup and cannot be changed afterward. This protects the age-based privacy rules that determine what information is visible on your profile.

### Is my data safe?

Yes. Arenalinq is COPPA-compliant for users under 13, and you control who sees your profile through [visibility settings](/privacy-and-visibility/visibility-settings). Your data is stored securely and is never sold.

### What's the difference between my login email and my public card email?

Your **login email** is the address you use to sign in (via the OTP code). The **"Show my email on my public card"** toggle in Settings controls whether that email is displayed on your public profile — it's a separate, opt-in choice.

### Can my parent still see my profile after I claim it?

Yes, but in **read-only mode**. After you claim your profile, your parent can still view it but can no longer make edits.
